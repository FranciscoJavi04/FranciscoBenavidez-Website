About Me

I was born and raised in Brownsville, Texas and graduated from Gladys Porter Early College High School in 2022. I’ve always been passionate about technology and how things work, which led me to pursue computer science. Over time, I discovered photography and videography as creative outlets that let me express ideas visually.

Throughout high school and college I’ve worked multiple jobs, starting as a restaurant host, then at McDonald’s, and later at Walmart in the entertainment department where I worked with electronics and photo services. Those roles strengthened my communication skills, technical knowledge, and ability to help people understand technology.

While my main career focus is computer science, photography remains a passion of mine. I built this website from scratch using GitHub Pages as both a portfolio and a resume to showcase my work, skills, and creative projects.
